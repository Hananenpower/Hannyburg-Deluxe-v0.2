# Hannyburg Deluxe Design Notes

## Story

Hannyburg is a neon coastal city controlled by Rafialdo. Hanny boi 123 starts downtown, finds his blue sports car, collects evidence, reaches Rafialdo Tower, and defeats Rafialdo in the final mission.

## Missions

1. Find the blue car.
2. Drive to the green garage marker.
3. Collect four evidence files around Hannyburg.
4. Reach Rafialdo Tower.
5. Final confrontation with Rafialdo.

## Graphics direction

This version uses a stylized low-poly look rather than blocks only:

- rounded heads
- body/arms/legs
- hair, eyes, shoes
- car body, windows, wheels, lights
- lit buildings with windows
- palm trees
- streetlights
- animated markers
- camera rotation
